MILA.HEALTH

It is 2041 and AI has taken over healthcare.

At MILA.HEALTH we are doing our bit to try and harness the new AI capabilities - without invading your privacy.

You just started video calling.

Watch as your video and audio data gets saved to your personal phone.

You just started answered your phone.

Watch as your audio data gets saved to your personal phone.

You just entered a CCTV area.

Watch as your video data gets saved to your personal phone.

It has been 24 hrs since insights were last generated.

First the saved audio data is used to check your BPM and breath rate.

Next, the video data gives us blood pressure and body temperature.

These vitals are added to the log of your vitals in phone memory.

These trends in vitals, along with your medical history can be be fed into the machine learning algorithm.

The algorithm then produces your updated propensity to a particular disease.

Add more people to the mix to see how your care can improve.

Your data is stored until 24 hrs since your last check and your phone procesor is idle.

Then, insights are generated.
